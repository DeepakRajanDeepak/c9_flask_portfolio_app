About

I firmly believe that any student can get a perfect score in the SAT/SAT Subjects/Advanced Placement with the right learning techniques. The approach I use during my one-on-one sessions is very dynamic and is extremely personalized. I don't have a standard lesson plan as such (A plan set in stone is incredibly inefficient - It is not much better than taking pre-recorded lessons online, in my opinion).

Here's a way of thinking about my approach - I look at the journey to a high test score as a voyage on a ship. However, the vessel isn't perfect - far from it - it has a lot of holes and defects. I step in with my students and fix these holes, starting with the biggest one (The student himself is often blind to these holes - otherwise, he would have fixed them already; Corresponds to huge score increases). Hence, every hour of work put by the student on his own is very effective and directly contributes to his score.

The ability to find these holes (and fix them effectively) stems from my excellent test scores, my extensive discussions with students (Both as a student myself and as an online tutor), and conversations with the co-founders of one of India's leading EdTech startup founders.

My scores in various standardised examinations are as follows: SAT With Essay: 1450/1600 (99th Percentile) (With an 800 in Math), SAT Physics:800/800, SAT Math level 2:800/800, AP Physics 1, Physics 2, Mechanics, Calculus BC, Calculus AB subscore, Electricity and magnetism: All 5/5, 12th Grade Board Exams(CBSE): 471/500, Joint Entrance Examination-Main (Physics nationwide percentile): 99.74, TOEFL: 106/120
